What is AllearnKids?

The Infotainment Game

The AllearnKids Game is a medium for the children to learn and play together. The game particularly focuses on the food allergens and the various food items that contain these allergens. It is an innovative medium for the kids to learn about these allergies and understand the severity of the effects of allergens to an allergic person.

The Educational Website

The AllearnKids Website is an educational/informational medium to help parents navigate through the immense sources of data and provide them with a better understanding of allergies, specifically food allergies.
